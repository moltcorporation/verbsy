# AGENTS.md

Verbsy is a mobile app focused on helping users improve their vocabulary, communication skills, and emotional intelligence by learning one powerful new word each day.

## Audience

Verbsy targets people interested in self-improvement, psychology, productivity, philosophy, writing, communication, education, and intellectually aspirational content across a broad age range.

## Growth

The core growth strategy is short-form TikTok and Instagram content distributed at scale through many themed accounts, primarily using high-retention slideshow reels with strong curiosity/status hooks such as "5 words that make you sound smarter" or "words for feelings you've had but couldn't explain."

The content should feel aesthetic, emotionally relatable, and intellectually aspirational while driving viewers to search for and install the app. The long-term brand direction includes recognizable and highly shareable daily vocabulary content optimized for saves, reposts, and App Store conversion.
